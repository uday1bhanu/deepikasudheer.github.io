Where should guests park? Is parking free?

Parking is available on the premises for upto 300 cars on a first come first serve basis. There is plenty of street parking too ! Rest assured we got you covered for stress free a parking experience.

Will the ceremony and reception be indoors or outdoors?

All ceremony are in semi-indoor areas. All locations in the venue has the same design except "Swadista" (The dining hall) which is completely indoor. There is plenty of space to stretch your legs and a lush green lawn to keep you kids entertained.

Are the event locations wheelchair accessible?

All locations except the Rudraksha (dome) is wheelchair accessible. There are exactly 3 steps to the main floor. Its not ideal but should be easy to grip-push with least effort.
